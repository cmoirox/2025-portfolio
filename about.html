Hi, I'm Roxane

I’m a digital designer originally from Brittany, France.

Passionate about computers and art, I specialized in digital creation and technology for 3 years.

After completing this Bachelor's degree, I decided to move to Paris to expand my skillset and pursue further studies.

I enrolled in the prestigious École Estienne for a Master’s degree in Design and Communication Strategy.

Why communication after digital design? Because combining the two fields feels like having a superpower.

During my Master’s, I had the opportunity to intern abroad. For 4 months, I was part of a team of motion designers and 3D artists.

In this studio/agency, I worked on projection mapping projects, developed metahumans connected to AI using Unreal Engine 5 in short, I fully immersed myself in digital creation serving brand storytelling.

Today, I continue combining technology with communication and branding strategy. I develop and code programs, work with various software, always seeking innovation, creating fresh ideas with my personal touch, and constantly eager to learn. :)
