FONTKROMA AI

FontKroma is a platform concept that uses AI for the creation of typographic characters. For this project, I had to use my programming skills: developing an AI model, and in artistic direction: visual identity, motion design, UI UX.

The main objective of this platform is to democratize typography for a wider audience through Artificial Intelligence. To do this, the platform allows users to generate, edit, learn about, and share typographic characters.

The result of several typographies obtained after multiple iterations of my AI model, which utilizes a generative model called CGAN.
