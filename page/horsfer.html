HORSFER LOCAL MEDIA

Horsfer is a local media with a musical vocation whose objective is to highlight passionate artists who often remain in the shadows. Horsfer strives to meet local talents, both men and women, who are not necessarily music professionals, to encourage collaborations among them. To achieve this, Horsfer conducts video interviews with its guests. After a moment of exchange, the artists have the opportunity to express themselves artistically.
